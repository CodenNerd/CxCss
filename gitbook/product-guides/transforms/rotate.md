### Rotate Classes

#### `.rotate-x-{number}`

Applies a 3D rotation around the x-axis to the element.

CSS equivalent: `transform: rotateX({number}deg);`

Example usage:
```html
<div class="rotate-x-45">This element is rotated 45 degrees around the x-axis.</div>
```

---
